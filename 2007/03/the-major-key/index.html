The Major Key

Mar 23rd, 2007

Patrick Wolf’s latest album The Magic Position is super-catchy eighties-throwback clap-along fun! Every time I hear the title track, I smile. The video is great, too, especially when he does his little double step at the beginning of the chorus. I think Wikipedia hit it on the head with their folktronica classification.
